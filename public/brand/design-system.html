Splash × Beam — the chosen direction

Design system.

The component vocabulary for slsh.me. Splash baseline (sticker energy, ink borders, marker highlights, hard-shadow card pops) with surgical Beam injections (gradient corner orbs on KPI tiles + feature cards). Migra carries the hero only — General Sans handles every other H2 / H3 / body. JetBrains Mono for slugs and code chips.

03 · Foundations · Typography

Three families, one rule.

Migra carries only the page hero h1. General Sans handles every H2, H3, body, button, label. JetBrains Mono for slugs, code, and meta. Italic Instrument Serif is permitted as a small accent on captions only.

04 · Foundations · Shape

Borders, corners, motion.

All shapes use ink for definition. Cards: 1.25px solid ink border + 3-4px hard-shadow offset (no soft drops). Tiles & chips: 1px translucent rule. Tilted elements straighten on hover (logo, hero mock, terminal, tilted cards). The empty state mark and brand mark share the same playful tilt vocabulary.
